The Thrill of Cooking and Coding

January 15, 2026

You caught me! I'm not a software engineer, or a data scientist, or a writer. What I am is someone desperate to learn, to have an outlet for some daily frustrations, and a space where I can write about what I want and how I want. I have been rejected promotions because I did not have the "technical skills", I have been rejected jobs because I did not have enough research experience. And, to deal with rejection, with bad grades, and any other trepidations life has to offer, oftentimes I cooked and hosted dinner parties, and dined out. Because in the modern world, where everything is behind a monitor, what makes us feel seen and human, are our senses. Nothing turns those senses on like food.

So what is it that I'm doing here? I am:

a. Learning to code (this website was created via GitHub and with the assistance of Claude)

b. Trying to regain the joy I felt when writing throughout college and high school.

c. Sharing with the world my thoughts on the digital age, and how food might help save us all.

Here, I will be conducting some data analysis on trends regarding our consumption of social media, as well as meals. Seeing what kind of history food carries and if there is anything we can bring into our day-to-day life to feel just a little bit better.
